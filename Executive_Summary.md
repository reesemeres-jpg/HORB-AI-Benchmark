# Executive Summary

Healthcare AI systems increasingly participate in workflows where safe decision-making depends on coordinating multiple evolving information sources rather than retrieving isolated facts.

The **Healthcare Operational Reasoning Benchmark Suite** is an original benchmark suite designed to evaluate operational reasoning across realistic healthcare workflows. The suite emphasizes prioritization, ownership boundaries, workflow dependencies, temporal reasoning, source provenance, and resource constraints while requiring AI systems to distinguish apparent operational readiness from actual executable readiness.

Rather than evaluating medical knowledge alone, the suite focuses on operational judgment in complex healthcare environments. Each benchmark simulates a short operational decision window and includes fictional source documents, system instructions, a task prompt, and strict grading guidelines.

The suite asks one central question:

> **Can an AI distinguish apparent operational readiness from actual executable readiness while making operationally sound decisions under realistic healthcare constraints?**
