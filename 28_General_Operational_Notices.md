# General Operational Notices

General L&D Operational Notices

- A clean LDR or Mother-Baby bed does not guarantee a safe move.
- Fetal monitoring changes should override older "likely admit" impressions until reviewed by the OB owner.
- A postpartum patient with recent heavier bleeding needs a stable reassessment before transfer.
- Scheduled inductions can be delayed when triage or OR risk changes.
- GBS status and allergy information affect induction readiness.
- Prior C-section with pain should be evaluated promptly before routine room moves consume staff.
- Decreased fetal movement needs timely monitoring space.
- Anesthesia cannot safely cover an epidural, an urgent OR setup, and another procedure at the same time.
- Families should not be given exact move or induction times until owner decisions close.
- Transport should receive one confirmed route, not multiple possible moves.
