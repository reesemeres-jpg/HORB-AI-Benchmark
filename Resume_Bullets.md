# Resume Bullets

## Independent Healthcare AI Evaluation Project

- Designed an original healthcare AI evaluation suite consisting of twelve multi-document operational reasoning benchmarks spanning labor and delivery, sterile processing, home health, outpatient infusion, hospice, emergency department triage, perioperative services, behavioral health, pharmacy transitions of care, case management, pre-admission testing, and primary care.
- Authored fictional operational source documents, structured evaluation prompts, system instructions, and detailed grading rubrics focused on operational reasoning under uncertainty.
- Developed a repeatable methodology for evaluating AI decision-making involving workflow dependencies, ownership boundaries, competing priorities, source provenance, and resource constraints.
- Built benchmark scenarios that evaluate whether AI systems distinguish apparent operational readiness from actual executable readiness in realistic healthcare workflows.
