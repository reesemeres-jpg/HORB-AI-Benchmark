# Why Nursing Matters

This benchmark suite was shaped by nursing experience.

Rather than emphasizing isolated factual questions, many scenarios reflect the cognitive demands of real healthcare operations:

- competing priorities
- incomplete information
- evolving workflow
- multiple operational stakeholders
- realistic resource constraints
- continual reprioritization

Many design decisions were influenced by the style of prioritization familiar from nursing education and bedside clinical practice, where several actions may be reasonable but one represents the best operational decision given the circumstances.

In that sense, the benchmarks evaluate more than whether AI knows healthcare terminology. They evaluate whether AI can navigate the cognitive load of healthcare operations.
