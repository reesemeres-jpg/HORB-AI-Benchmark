# About Meredith Reese, RN

I'm Meredith Reese, RN.

This project began as an independent exploration of AI behavior while interacting with large language models during my own time. As the project evolved, I became increasingly interested in how AI systems perform when operational healthcare decisions require prioritization under uncertainty rather than straightforward factual recall.

Drawing on my nursing background, I developed an original benchmark suite centered on operational reasoning across multiple healthcare environments.

## Areas of Interest

- Healthcare AI evaluation
- Benchmark design
- Operational reasoning
- Clinical workflow modeling
- Human-AI collaboration
- Model quality assessment
