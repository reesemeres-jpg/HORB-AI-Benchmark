# Project Scope & Limitations

## Purpose

The Healthcare Operational Reasoning Benchmark Suite was developed to evaluate how AI systems perform when operational decisions require integrating multiple evolving information sources under realistic healthcare workflow constraints.

The suite is intended to assess operational reasoning rather than factual recall or medical knowledge alone.

## Scope

This benchmark suite evaluates an AI system's ability to:

- Reason across multiple operational documents simultaneously.
- Distinguish apparent readiness from executable operational readiness.
- Respect workflow ownership and decision boundaries.
- Prioritize among competing operational demands.
- Manage uncertainty when information is incomplete or evolving.
- Produce operationally realistic recommendations rather than simply identifying isolated facts.

## What This Project Does Not Evaluate

This benchmark suite is not intended to evaluate:

- Diagnostic accuracy.
- Clinical treatment recommendations.
- Medical knowledge recall.
- Guideline memorization.
- Board-style examination performance.
- Clinical competence independent of operational context.

Instead, the suite focuses specifically on operational reasoning within healthcare workflows.

## Fictional Content

All benchmark materials are fictional and were created solely for evaluation and portfolio purposes. No real patients, health records, healthcare organizations, or confidential clinical information are included.

## Current Limitations

This project represents an independent benchmark design effort and should be understood within that context.

Current limitations include:

- The benchmark suite has not yet undergone formal inter-rater reliability studies.
- Benchmark difficulty has been refined through iterative design rather than large-scale statistical validation.
- Performance analyses across multiple AI models are outside the scope of this portfolio.
- Future revisions may expand domain coverage, improve rubric calibration, and incorporate formal validation methodologies.
